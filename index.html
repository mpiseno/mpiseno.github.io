About Me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Stanford University advised by Karen Liu. Previously, I graduated summa cum laude from Georgia Tech with a BS in Computer Science and minor in Mathematics.

I work on robotics and machine learning. My research is supported by the National Defense Science and Engineering Graduate (NDSEG) Fellowship.
